About me

My name is François Deloche. I am a PhD student at the CAMS (Centre d'analyse et de mathématique sociales ), EHESS (Paris) under the direction of Jean-Pierre Nadal.
I'm part of the project SpeechCODE. We seek to understand how the infants encode the sound for speech detection and recognition. I also work on a project of speech enhancement for auditory protheses.
I'm a former student of the master MVA (applied mathematics program on statistics, machine learning and signal processing).
